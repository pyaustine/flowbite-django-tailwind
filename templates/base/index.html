Jijue development was carried out by a passionate team

Jijue is a website created by a multidisciplinary team of students from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. The platform's main goal is to provide a user-friendly website for individuals to assess their HIV risk and connect them to health facilities for further management.

In today's fast-paced world, Jijue was created to provide a smart, accessible, and multifunctional digital health platform. It offers a seamless experience in undertaking an accurate self-risk assessment for HIV while creating a nationwide linkage for easy accessibility to HIV testing and prevention services. Jijue is striving to reshape the approach in promoting HIV testing and awareness.

By providing access to information and resources, Jijue hopes to empower individuals to take control of their health and make informed decisions about their HIV risk. With their innovative approach to promoting HIV testing and awareness, Jijue is committed to making a difference in the fight against HIV/AIDS.

What's unique about Jijue?

Jijue is a cutting-edge HIV risk assessment tool that stands out from other risk calculators. Our machine learning model, personalized recommendations, and user-friendly interface make Jijue a unique and valuable resource for anyone concerned about their HIV risk.

01

Cutting-edge Machine Learning Model

Cutting-edge Machine Learning Model

Jijue is powered by a state-of-the-art machine learning model that is based on the latest scientific research. This model is able to accurately predict an individual's risk of contracting HIV based on a number of factors, including demographics, sexual history, and drug use. Our model is constantly updated with new data to ensure that it remains accurate and reliable.

02

Personalized Recommendations

Personalized Recommendations

Jijue provides personalized recommendations to help individuals reduce their risk of HIV transmission. Based on an individual's risk assessment, Jijue will provide tailored advice on testing, prevention, and treatment options. These recommendations are designed to be accessible and easy to understand, so that individuals can take action to protect their health.

03

User-Friendly Interface

User-Friendly Interface

Jijue has been designed with the user in mind. Our website is easy to navigate and provides clear instructions on how to use the tool. We have worked hard to ensure that the website is accessible to everyone, regardless of their level of technical expertise. Our goal is to provide a seamless user experience that allows individuals to quickly and easily assess their HIV risk and take action to protect themselves.
