What does it mean to be Low Risk

This means that you are less likely to have been exposed to HIV based on the risk assessment.
To confirm your HIV status, you can get tested by:

  • Booking an appointment to visit the nearest VCT centre
  • Ordering a self-test kit and test yourself at the comfort of your home

You can remain at low risk by;

  • Practicing safe sex by consistent and correct condom use.
  • Avoiding usage of already-used needle/syringe
  • Frequent screening for sexually transmitted infections .
  • Continually educate yourself on HIV transmission.

Prevention of Mother-to-Child Transmission

All women of reproductive age should be on contraceptive use to prevent unplanned pregnancies. You are advised to have frequent HIV and pregnancy self-test
