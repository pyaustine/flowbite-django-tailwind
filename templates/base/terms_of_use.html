Introduction

Jijue website was created by a multidisciplinary team of students from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. The aim of the web app is to provide a user-friendly platform for individuals to assess their HIV risk and connect them to Health Facilities for further management. The platform offers users the ability to access testing for HIV and other STIs; and links users to services, including PrEP, through networked service providers.
